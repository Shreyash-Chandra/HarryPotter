Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows - Part 2

The second part continues with Harry, Ron, and Hermione's search for the remaining Horcruxes. They break into Gringotts Wizarding Bank to retrieve another Horcrux and then return to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, which is now under Voldemort's control. They unite with their friends and members of the Order of the Phoenix to defend the school and its students.

A major battle ensues between Voldemort's forces and the defenders of Hogwarts. Harry discovers that he himself is an unintentional Horcrux, and he sacrifices himself to destroy the piece of Voldemort's soul within him. However, he is not truly dead, as he is transported to a sort of limbo where he meets Dumbledore.

Harry learns that he needs to return to the battle and destroy the remaining Horcruxes. He returns to Hogwarts, faces Voldemort, and reveals that he is still alive. A final showdown between Harry and Voldemort takes place, and Harry ultimately defeats Voldemort, destroying him once and for all.

The film ends with an epilogue set 19 years later, showing Harry, Ron, and Hermione as adults, sending their own children off to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

This movie conclude the Harry Potter film series, wrapping up the story of Harry's journey from an orphaned child to the vanquisher of the Dark Lord.
